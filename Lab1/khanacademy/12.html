Fluffy Pancakes

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 2-3
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 1 cup
Milk 3/4 cup
Egg 1
Baking Powder 1 tsp
Sugar 1 tbsp
Salt 1/4 tsp

Steps:

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. Whisk in milk and egg until smooth batter forms.
  3. Heat a non-stick pan and pour small amounts of batter to form pancakes.
  4. Cook until bubbles form, flip, and cook another 1-2 minutes.
  5. Serve warm with syrup, fruit, or toppings of choice.

Spaghetti Bolognese

  • Time: 40 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti 400 g
Ground Beef 300 g
Tomato Sauce 1 cup
Onion 1, chopped
Garlic 2 cloves, minced
Olive Oil 2 tbsp

Steps:

  1. Boil spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan, saut√© onion and garlic until soft.
  3. Add ground beef, cook until browned.
  4. Stir in tomato sauce and simmer 15-20 minutes.
  5. Serve sauce over spaghetti and enjoy!
